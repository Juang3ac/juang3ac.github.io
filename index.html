About him

Dr. Isaac Asimov (2 January 1920 – 6 April 1992) was a Russian-born American Jewish author and biochemist, a highly successful and exceptionally prolific writer best known for his works of science fiction and for his many non-fiction books, including several genres: popular science writings, guides to the bible and science, mystery novels, literature commentary (his works on Shakespeare) and history.

I decided to make it from Isaac Asimov, one of my favorite Sci-fi writers. Not the favorite, but at least the one who introduced me to the genre and made me a fan for life (so far). With its stories, is possible to notice that he was a man ahead of his time. For example...

Asimov was the first in history that used the word "robotics". In its shorts stories of "I, Robot", he stated for the first time the possible ethical problems that AI could have with their decision making process.

In "Foundation" (published 1951), he created a fictional science branch called "Psychohistory", that is a science capable of making general predictions of large groups of population; Psychohistory is capable to predict thanks to all the data compiled from long periods of time.
